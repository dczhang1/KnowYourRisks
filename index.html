About the Project

The "Know Your Risks" project investigates the factors that influence risk-taking behaviors in workplace environments, with the goal of developing effective interventions to improve safety outcomes.

Our interdisciplinary team combines expertise in behavioral science, organizational psychology, and safety engineering to create a comprehensive understanding of risk perception and decision-making.

Research Focus Areas

Risk Perception

Investigating how individuals identify and evaluate workplace hazards across different industries and roles.

Organizational Factors

Examining how leadership, culture, and policies influence safety behaviors and outcomes.

Decision-Making Processes

Understanding the cognitive and emotional factors that impact risk-based decisions in time-sensitive situations.

Intervention Design

Developing and testing evidence-based approaches to enhance risk awareness and improve safety behaviors.
