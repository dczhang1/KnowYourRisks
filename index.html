Understanding the Science of Risk Takers at Work

Funded by the National Science Foundation, our research project explores why employees take risks at work and what these choices mean for organizations. Through a multi-disciplinary and multi-method approach, we examine the psychology behind workplace risk-taking. Our scientifically validated tools enable employees and managers to understand their unique risk preferences and behaviors. Discover your risk profile with our free self-assessments and gain insights that can transform workplace decision-making.
