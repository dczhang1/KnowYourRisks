Risk Assessment Questionnaires

Choose a questionnaire to assess your risk-taking profile

General Risk Propensity Scale (GRiPS)

Measures your overall tendency to take risks across various domains of life.

Time: 10-15 minutes

GRiPS Details

This comprehensive assessment explores your risk-taking tendencies across multiple life domains, including:

  • Financial decisions
  • Personal relationships
  • Career choices
  • Leisure activities
Start Assessment

Calculated and Spontaneous Risk Taking Scale (CASPRT)

Assesses your approach to risk-taking, differentiating between calculated and spontaneous risks.

Time: 15-20 minutes

CASPRT Details

This unique assessment distinguishes between:

  • Calculated, strategic risks
  • Impulsive, spontaneous risks
  • Decision-making patterns
  • Risk perception
Start Assessment

Everyday Risky Choice at Work Inventory (ERIK)

Evaluates your risk-taking behavior specifically in workplace scenarios.

Time: 20-25 minutes

ERIK Details

A targeted assessment focusing on workplace risk behaviors, including:

  • Decision-making under pressure
  • Professional risk tolerance
  • Organizational risk perception
  • Strategic vs. reactive choices
